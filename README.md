# Problem Definition
**Student-Management-System**
**- A full-stack Student Management System designed to manage student data, attendance, and academic records efficiently.

**Student Management System — Task Module**

**Background**
- In academic environments, students are regularly assigned various tasks such as assignments, quizzes, and projects. Without a centralized system, students often struggle to keep track of their pending, ongoing, and completed tasks, leading to missed deadlines and poor academic performance.

**Problem Statement**
- Students currently lack an efficient way to organize, monitor, and manage their academic tasks. There is no unified platform where both students and teachers can create, track, and update task information in real time. This results in disorganization, difficulty in prioritizing workloads, and reduced academic productivity.

**Proposed Solution**
- The proposed system is a web-based Student Task Management module that allows teachers to create and assign tasks (assignments, quizzes, projects), and enables students to view, track, and update the status of their tasks (pending, ongoing, completed). The system will support full CRUD operations, task filtering, user roles, and secure login to ensure an organized and accessible academic workflow.

**Stakeholders**
- Students
- Teachers / Faculty
